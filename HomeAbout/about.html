Welcome to Weblectric
Ireland's online music festival
-----------------------------

We may not be able to rave together on the beautiful fields of Ireland right now, but don't give up hope. That's why we're here! (and you, too)

We want you to let loose and dance the 2020 cobwebs clean out of your system, be it in your kitchen or your garden, with your mum, sister or you're best festival pal! We are bringing Ireland's groovy festival community together to shake it up once more, 2021 style.

Live streaming
BRAND NEW TRACKS
from your favourite artists, direct from our studio to your sound system.

So plug in, turn it up, grab a drink and lets boogie (2019 vibes is that you??).

For exclusive content, access to our virtual itinerary and to be included in a chance to win a pair of brand new BEATIT headphones, subscribe to our mailing list!
